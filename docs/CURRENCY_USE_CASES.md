## üöÄ Creative Use Cases for Tokens

### 1. **Token-Based Features & Services**

#### **Card Duplication Service** ‚≠ê HIGH PRIORITY
- **Description**: Use tokens to duplicate cards you already own
- **Cost**: 5 tokens = 1 duplicate of any card in your collection
- **Use Case**: Get duplicates for upgrading/fusing, or collect multiple variants
- **Impact**: Gives players control over what they get

#### **Card Reroll/Refund** ‚≠ê MEDIUM PRIORITY
- **Description**: Reroll a card you just got (get a different card)
- **Cost**: 3 tokens per reroll
- **Limit**: Can only reroll within 1 minute of getting the card
- **Use Case**: Don't like the card you got? Spend tokens to get another
- **Impact**: Reduces frustration from bad rolls

#### **Rate-Up Banner Access** ‚≠ê HIGH PRIORITY
- **Description**: Special banners with increased rates for specific characters/anime
- **Cost**: 2 tokens per roll (higher cost, better rates)
- **Features**: 
  - Featured characters have 2x drop rate
  - Limited-time banners (e.g., "Summer Banner", "Valentine's Banner")
  - Banner-specific guaranteed pulls
- **Impact**: Creates excitement and targeted spending

#### **Token Betting System** ‚≠ê LOW PRIORITY
- **Description**: Bet tokens on roll outcomes
- **Cost**: 1 token to place a bet
- **Examples**:
  - "Bet you'll get a 4+ star in next 5 rolls" (win: +5 tokens, lose: -1 token)
  - "Bet you'll get a prismatic in next 20 rolls" (win: +50 tokens, lose: -1 token)
- **Impact**: Adds risk/reward element

#### **Token Gifting** ‚≠ê MEDIUM PRIORITY
- **Description**: Send tokens to friends (if friend system exists)
- **Cost**: 1 token = 1 gift token
- **Limit**: 10 tokens per day can be gifted
- **Impact**: Social engagement and community building
